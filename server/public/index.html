Category endpoints

  • Get all categories localhost:4000/ api/ categories/ Query: page=1+ | step=15+ | sortBy=date.desc / date.asc
  • Get category by its Mongo ObjectId localhost:4000/ api/ category/ :categoryId/
  • Get subcategories in category by its Mongo ObjectId localhost:4000/ api/ category/ :categoryId/ subcategories Query: page=1+ | step=15+ | sortBy=date.desc / date.asc
  • Get posts in subcategory by its Mongo ObjectId localhost:4000/ api/ category/ :categoryId/ posts Query: page=1+ | step=15+ | sortBy=date.desc / date.asc

    Post endpoints

  • Get all posts localhost:4000/ api/ posts/ Query: page=1+ | step=15+ | sortBy=date.desc / date.asc
  • Get post by its Mongo ObjectId localhost:4000/ api/ post/ :postId/
  • Get author in post by its Mongo ObjectId localhost:4000/ api/ post/ :postId/ author
  • Get thank list in post by its Mongo ObjectId localhost:4000/ api/ post/ :postId/ thanks

    User endpoints

  • Get user by its Mongo ObjectId localhost:4000/ api/ user/ :userId/
